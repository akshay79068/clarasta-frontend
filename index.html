FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a tutor match?+
Most parents receive a match within 2–4 hours of registering. In many cases it happens within 30 minutes. You'll get the tutor's name, college, and WhatsApp directly.
Is Clarasta free to use?+
100% free — for both parents and tutors. We don't charge registration fees, subscription fees, or any commission. Parents pay tutors directly via UPI.
How are tutors verified?+
Tutors submit their college ID card on WhatsApp. Our team manually verifies each ID within 24–48 hours before making the profile visible to parents.
What if I'm not happy with my tutor?+
Just WhatsApp us — no questions asked. We'll find a replacement tutor within 24 hours at zero cost. Your satisfaction is our guarantee.
How much do tutors charge per hour?+
Rates typically range from ₹200–₹400/hr depending on class level. Class 9–12 averages ₹300/hr. JEE/NEET tutors charge ₹400–₹600/hr. All agreed directly with the tutor.
Can a tutor teach multiple subjects?+
Yes! Many BTech tutors handle Maths, Physics, and Chemistry together. You can specify multiple subjects when registering and we'll match accordingly.
