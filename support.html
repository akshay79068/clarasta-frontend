👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents

Everything you need to know about finding and hiring a tutor through Clarasta.

How do I find a tutor near my society?+
Go to the Find Tutor section, type your society name (e.g., "Crossing Republic" or "Gaur City"), and filter by subject and class. You'll see verified tutors who live in or near your area. Alternatively, register as a parent and our team will match you personally within 2–4 hours via WhatsApp.
Is there a free demo class?+
Yes, absolutely! Every Clarasta tutor offers a free 30–45 minute demo class. This lets you and your child evaluate the tutor before committing. If you're not satisfied, we'll match you with another tutor at no cost.
How quickly will I get a match?+
Most parents receive a tutor match within 2–4 hours of registering. In some areas we're able to match within 30 minutes if tutors are available. You'll receive the tutor's details directly on WhatsApp.
Can I request a specific subject or class combination?+
Yes. Fill in your child's class and the subject during registration. If you need multiple subjects or want a tutor for multiple children, message us on WhatsApp — we'll find the best match for your specific needs.
What if I'm not happy with my tutor?+
Just message us on WhatsApp — no questions asked. We'll find you a replacement tutor within 24 hours. Your satisfaction is our priority, and there's no penalty for switching tutors.
💡 Pro Tip: For the fastest match, register with your exact society name and tower number. Tutors who live in the same building or within a 500m radius are prioritized.

🎓 For Tutors

Everything BTech students need to know to start earning through Clarasta.

How do I register as a tutor?+
Go to auth.html, select "I'm a Tutor", fill in your details including college, branch, subjects, and the society where you can teach. Your profile will be reviewed and verified within 24–48 hours.
What documents are needed for verification?+
Just your college ID card — front side only. Send it on WhatsApp to our verification team after registering. We verify that you are an active enrolled student before making your profile visible to parents.
How much can I earn per month?+
Typical earnings depend on your schedule:

• 2 students, 1 hr/day → ₹2,400–₹3,200/month
• 3 students, 1.5 hrs/day → ₹5,400–₹6,000/month
• 4+ students → ₹8,000–₹12,000/month

Rates typically range ₹250–₹400/hr depending on class level. JEE/NEET tutors earn ₹400–₹600/hr.
Does Clarasta take a commission from tutors?+
Zero commission. Ever. Clarasta is a free platform. Parents pay you directly on a monthly basis. We never deduct from your earnings or charge you any fees. Our revenue model does not involve taking a cut from tutors or parents.
How do parents contact me once I'm verified?+
Once your profile is live, parents can see it in the tutor listings and contact you directly on your registered WhatsApp number. You'll also receive introductions from our team when we manually match you with parents who registered with specific requirements.
✅ Fastest to get started: After registering, send your college ID to our WhatsApp immediately. Tutors who submit their ID within the same day typically get verified and go live within 4–6 hours.

💰 Pricing & Fees

Transparent, fair, and platform-fee-free pricing.

How much does Clarasta charge?+
Clarasta is 100% free — for both parents and tutors. We do not charge registration fees, subscription fees, platform commissions, or any hidden charges. Parents pay tutors directly, and Clarasta earns nothing from those transactions.
What are typical tutor rates in my area?+
Typical hourly rates by class:

• Class 1–5: ₹150–₹250/hr
• Class 6–8: ₹200–₹300/hr
• Class 9–10: ₹250–₹350/hr
• Class 11–12: ₹300–₹450/hr
• JEE/NEET/Competitive: ₹400–₹600/hr

Rates are agreed directly between the parent and tutor — Clarasta does not set or regulate them.
How do payments work?+
Parents pay tutors directly — typically monthly via UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm). Clarasta is not involved in any payment transactions. This keeps things simple and direct, with no delays or middlemen.

✅ Verification Process

How we ensure every tutor on Clarasta is genuine and trustworthy.

How does Clarasta verify tutors?+
Our 3-step verification process:

1. Tutor submits profile with college and branch details
2. Tutor sends college ID card on WhatsApp
3. Our team manually reviews the ID and activates the profile

Tutors are only visible to parents after all 3 steps are complete.
How long does verification take?+
Typically 24–48 hours after submitting your college ID. If you submit during business hours (10 AM – 8 PM), we usually verify within 4–6 hours. You'll receive a WhatsApp confirmation once your profile is live.
What if a tutor turns out to be fake or unreliable?+
Report immediately on WhatsApp. We'll remove the tutor's profile within 2 hours of a complaint. Parents' WhatsApp numbers are never shared publicly — only tutors can be contacted by parents, not the other way around.

🔒 Safety & Trust

Your family's safety is our top priority.

Is it safe to allow a tutor into my home?+
Clarasta tutors are college-verified BTech students from colleges like ABES Engineering, Galgotias, KIET, Sharda University, etc. All live in nearby societies — making them accountable, traceable, and local. We strongly recommend conducting the free demo class first before committing, and always meeting the tutor in a common area initially.
Is my personal information safe?+
Your WhatsApp number and address are only shared with tutors you specifically choose to connect with. Parent details from registration are stored securely and never sold or shared with third parties. Tutors' WhatsApp numbers are only shown after a match is confirmed.
What colleges are Clarasta tutors from?+
Our tutors come from colleges that are walking distance from Crossing Republic, Gaur City, and nearby areas, including: ABES Engineering College, Galgotias University, KIET Group of Institutions, Sharda University, GL Bajaj Institute, and Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology (RKGIT).
