Exam Guide

Major Entrance Exams — Complete Guide

Kaunsa exam, kab dena hai, kaise taiyari kare — sabka honest breakdown.

JEE Main
Engineering
Joint Entrance Examination (Main)
🎯NITs, IIITs, GFTIs + JEE Advanced eligibility
📅January & April (2 attempts per year)
📝Maths + Physics + Chemistry — 90 MCQ, 3 hours
✅Qualifying marks: 85+ percentile for top NITs
💡 Strategy: NCERT pehle 2 baar pado. Previous 10 years papers must. Classes 11+12 dono equally important.
JEE Advanced
IITs
Joint Entrance Examination (Advanced)
🎯23 IITs — India ka most prestigious engineering exam
📅May/June (JEE Main top 2.5L hi appear ho sakte hain)
📝2 Papers × 3 hours — deep concept-based questions
✅Rank under 5000 chahiye good IIT branch ke liye
💡 Strategy: Problem-solving aur deep understanding critical. Mock tests regularly do. 2 saal minimum prep needed.
NEET-UG
Medical
National Eligibility cum Entrance Test
🎯MBBS, BDS, BAMS, Nursing — all govt & pvt colleges
📅May each year — single attempt
📝Biology (90Q) + Physics (45Q) + Chemistry (45Q) = 720 marks
✅AIIMS ke liye 650+, Govt college ke liye 550+
💡 Strategy: Biology NCERT line-by-line — koi bhi line mat chhodna! Previous year questions most important resource hai.
CUET-UG
All Streams
Common University Entrance Test
🎯DU, BHU, JNU aur 250+ central universities
📅May/June each year
📝NCERT-based domain subjects + English + GK
✅DU ke popular courses mein 97–99% percentile needed
💡 Strategy: Class 12 NCERT main resource. Speed aur accuracy matter karti hai — time management practice karo.
CLAT
Law
Common Law Admission Test
🎯24 National Law Universities (NLUs) — NLSIU, NLU Delhi top hai
📅December each year
📝English, GK, Legal Reasoning, Quant, Logical — 120 Qs
✅Top 5 NLU ke liye 100+ marks out of 120
💡 Strategy: Daily newspaper reading must (Hindu/IE). Legal reasoning comprehension based hoti hai — reading speed improve karo.
CAT
MBA
Common Admission Test
🎯IIMs aur 1000+ MBA colleges in India
📅November each year — graduation ke baad dena hoga
📝VARC + DILR + Quantitative Aptitude — 66 Qs, 2 hours
✅IIM-A/B/C ke liye 99+ percentile, top IIMs 95+
💡 Strategy: Any stream/degree eligible. 2 years of serious prep recommended for IIM. Mocks aur analysis most important.
